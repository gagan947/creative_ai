Why Creative Studio?

Make your dream apps reality

When we create an app we stack the features you need using re-usable, Lego-like building blocks, saving you time and money. Then we make all the customisations you want so your app is uniquely yours. And you never need to speak to a developer or agency.

Guaranteed pricing and clear timelines

We charge per feature, with everything defined in a pre-agreed Build Card before development starts. Plus we give you accurate timelines, meaning there is less of a chance of things spiraling out of control down the line.

How our pricing works

Helping your apps to succeed (and scale)

We don’t just abandon you when your project is complete. Instead, our Customer Success team gives you personalised guidance to ensure you get the most out of our products and services, while our aftercare plan, Studio One, ensures your software stays up-to-date and bug free.

You keep your code

Unlike most software platforms, we give you your code to keep. We offer comprehensive app maintenance (your first year is included in your build price) but there’s no lock in to stay after that. You have total flexibility to scale.

Free app prototype

Want to visualise your app? Then we’ve got you covered. You can share your prototype with internal teams and investors to make your pitch even more powerful. And they’re totally free.
