You’ll always get a transparent app price.

After our founder got burned by developers while trying to create a photo-sharing app, he decided to shake up the
process. Here are some of the key principles he implemented to make app development simpler, more efficient and more
transparent.

No hidden costs

Unlike software development agencies, which often charge per hour, our feature-based pricing model allows us to guarantee the price of your app up front.

That means if it takes us longer than we expected to develop your features, you don’t get punished.

Keep your code

Unlike the pricing model used by no-code app builders – where you rent your app’s code for as long as it’s in use – you keep your code when your 24-month payment plan is complete.

This means you’re in total control of your app’s future development, rather than being reliant on a third party.

Cloud savings

We take on long-term contracts with the big cloud providers – including Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure and Digital Ocean – so you don’t have to.

Plus, you can monitor your spend and optimise your capacity via your live Cloud dashboard.

1 year of free app maintenance

Because this is included in your Buildcard as standard, there’s no need to pay for extra third-party aftercare services when the app development process is over.

All you need to do is focus on growing your business.
